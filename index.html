ZAINAB HUSENI JABLA

THE ARTIST

About Me

I was about 6 years old when I first began painting. I used to spoil all of my mothers paints and fill the empty bottles with water.
This was my usual past time when my parents went out or by any chance I was alone at home. I painted the carpets, curtains and my school books.
Finally my mom decided to put into a painting class and that’s when my journey began.

I was fast in learning the techniques and finished the course in 1 month which usually took a month and a half. In this class I began with acrylic paints and soon after
I was done with this course I bought a few cheap oil paints. I was always attracted towards oil colors.Later I decided to join an oil painting class.That’s how my journey
truly began!


OIL PAINTING ARTIST

Zainab is a contemporary realist painter based in Toronto. Just take a look at my sample portfolio on the website and you'll soon see the amazing attention to detail in my paintings!

Hello

Tyra Pinto
Digital Designer

View Resume


I’m Tyra, a Masters student at Ryerson University studying Digital Media. I am interested in UX/UI Design, Storytelling, and Interdisciplinary Innovation, Transmedia, and Interaction Design Experiences.

About

During Tyra’s undergraduate degree, she pushed herself to build a career in the music industry. While graduating during the start of a global pandemic, she realized that getting a job was going to be impossible. So, she decided to continue her studies and dive into something she always wanted to try, but was too afraid to. Digital Media and Design had always struck her interest, and now she is finally doing it!

During her academic journey she has shown interest in using digital platforms to create positive change. With having all the resources and technology at our fingertips, she believes that more can be done to better our realities, rather than using technology and media to escape it.

Digital Media can change the world. We interact with the media everyday, so I believe it has the power to change lives. Our current digital world provides us with endless possibilities that also have the power to influence people. I want to use the skills I gain in this program to create positive change. I am passionate about improving, learning and growing. I want to be a resource that can lift people up. It is such a privilege to be in this position, so I want to push myself to innovate and create projects, and products that are backed by compassion for the greater good.
